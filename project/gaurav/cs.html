Company Secretary (CS)

If you are wondering what to do after b.com, Company Secretary (CS) is one of the many pivotal job roles in an organization. A CS is responsible for managing all the legal aspects of a firm/organization. Company Secretaries usually take care of a company’s tax returns, maintain tax records, offer actionable advice to the board of directors, and ensure that all the legal and statutory regulations are met with and one of the best courses after b.com. To become a Company Secretary, you must study corporate law. This is a 3-year long degree comprises of three stages – Foundation, Intermediate, and Finals. The salary range of CS in India ranges between Rs 4 – 10 LPA. According to PayScale the average salary of CS is Rs. 5.8 LPA.
