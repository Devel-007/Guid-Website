Science

Courses in Science

For those who have studied Science with Maths (PCM/PCMB) in Class XI and XII, the most popular courses are
1.BTech/BE
2.Integrated MTech
3.BCA
4.BArch
5.BSc
Students with PCM/PCMB combination can also make a career in the aviation industry as a Commercial Pilot, and are also eligible to get into the National Defence Academy (NDA), Pune.

For students who have studied Science with Biology (PCB/PCMB) in Class XI and XII, the most popular courses are 1.MBBS
2.BDS
3.BPharma
4.Nursing
5.Paramedical
To find out more about the course highlights, list of colleges offering that course, college rankings, college reviews, top specializations, details of entrance exams required, and frequently asked questions, click on the links in the table below.

Science students are also eligible to pursue courses beyond the Science stream, including courses in
1.Law
2.Management
3.Mass Communication
4.Animation
5.Graphic Designing
6.Travel & Tourism
7.Hospitality
8.Fashion Designing
To pursue most of the courses after class XII, a candidate has to appear in common entrance tests, conducted either by government bodies or private institutes.
